# BAWISA
Website development for Bay Area Women in Aerospace

# Who we are
Bay Area Women in Space and Aerospace (BAWISA) is a quarterly speaker series connecting and inspiring women and gender minorities in aerospace. Since 2016, we've hosted events at startups, research labs, and incubators across the Bay Area, covering everything from founding startups and negotiation to data analytics, self-branding, and company features.
Our mission is to connect and inspire women and gender minorities in aerospace, helping with hiring, retention, and community in a historically male-dominated field.
